DeFi Protocol Development

Project Overview

SteeLab developed a comprehensive money market protocol on NEAR blockchain, enabling users to lend, borrow, and earn interest on cryptocurrency assets. The platform combines algorithmic interest rate models, cross-collateralization, and advanced risk management to create a sustainable DeFi ecosystem.

The Challenge

Building a secure and efficient money market required addressing critical DeFi challenges:

  • Designing sustainable interest rate models that balance supply and demand
  • Implementing robust liquidation mechanisms to prevent systemic risk
  • Creating gas-efficient smart contracts for high-frequency operations
  • Building accurate price oracles with manipulation resistance
  • Ensuring protocol solvency during market volatility
  • Developing user-friendly interfaces for complex DeFi operations
  • Implementing governance for decentralized protocol upgrades

Our Solution

SteeLab delivered a battle-tested DeFi protocol with institutional-grade features:

  • Algorithmic Interest Rates: Implemented dynamic rate curves based on utilization ratios and market conditions
  • Multi-Asset Collateral: Developed cross-collateralization system supporting 20+ digital assets
  • Automated Liquidation: Created efficient liquidation engine with keeper incentives
  • Oracle Integration: Implemented Pyth Network and Chainlink price feeds with failover mechanisms
  • Flash Loan Protection: Built safeguards against flash loan attacks and price manipulation
  • Yield Optimization: Developed auto-compounding strategies for lenders
  • DAO Governance: Implemented on-chain voting for protocol parameters and upgrades

Technical Implementation

The protocol leverages NEAR's performance and developer-friendly features:

  • Rust smart contracts optimized for NEAR Protocol
  • Compound-style interest accrual with per-block calculations
  • ERC-4626 tokenized vault standard for lending positions
  • Multi-signature treasury with timelock controls
  • Subgraph indexing for real-time protocol analytics
  • React/Next.js frontend with Web3 wallet integration
  • Automated testing suite with Hardhat and Foundry
  • Comprehensive monitoring with Defender and OpenZeppelin

Results & Impact

The money market protocol achieved remarkable growth and stability:

  • $150M+ Total Value Locked (TVL) within 6 months
  • 25,000+ active users across 45 countries
  • $500M+ in cumulative lending volume
  • Average APY of 8-15% for lenders
  • Zero protocol exploits or security breaches
  • 99.9% liquidation success rate during market crashes
  • Listed on CoinGecko and DeFi Llama top protocols

Key Features Delivered

  • Supply and earn interest on 20+ supported assets
  • Borrow against collateral with flexible LTV ratios
  • Isolated lending pools for risk segregation
  • Flash loans for arbitrage and liquidation bots
  • Liquidation protection with health factor monitoring
  • One-click leverage strategies (up to 5x)
  • Real-time analytics dashboard with APY tracking
  • Mobile-responsive dApp with MetaMask integration

Risk Management

  • Collateral Factors: Conservative LTV ratios (60-75%) based on asset volatility
  • Liquidation Incentives: 8% bonus for liquidators to ensure timely debt repayment
  • Reserve Factor: 10% protocol fee allocated to insurance fund
  • Interest Rate Caps: Maximum borrow rates to prevent excessive leverage
  • Oracle Security: Multiple price sources with median aggregation

Security Measures

  • Audited by CertiK, Quantstamp, and PeckShield
  • $1M bug bounty program on Immunefi
  • Formal verification of core protocol logic
  • Emergency pause mechanism for critical situations
  • Timelock delays for governance proposals (48 hours)
  • Regular penetration testing and security reviews
